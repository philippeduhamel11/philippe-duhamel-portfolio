COMMERCIAL INDIE GAME

BIBOTS

Bibots is a top-down roguelike shooter in which players explore procedurally generated dungeons by combining weapons, powers, and mechanical creatures in a visceral, arcade-style gameplay experience.

DURATION

2018 — 2023

PRODUCTION

End-to-end development of a debut independent commercial game, from prototype to release. Published by Plug In Digital and supported by Pictanovo.

RESPONSIBILITIES
  • System & Gameplay Programming
  • Game & Level Design
  • Design & Integration UI
  • Art & VFX Integration
  • Production & Polish
