GAME JAM PROJECT

SAWRRY NOT SORRY

Take on the role of trees that tear each other apart by throwing chainsaws at one another in this party game for 2 to 4 players.

DURATION

OCTOBER 2020

PRODUCTION

Creation of a party game by a team of three. Developed in two weeks using Unity for the Disc Room game jam.

RESPONSIBILITIES

  • Gameplay programming

  • Animations & assets integration
